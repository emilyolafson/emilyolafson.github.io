I am a third year PhD candidate in the Weill Cornell Medicine Neuroscience department advised by Amy Kuceyeski of the CoCo lab. I am interested in using neuroimaging to understand and predict recovery from brain injury. Currently, my research is focused on using functional MRI to capture functional plasticity after stroke.

Before coming to Weill Cornell I completed a BSc in Neuroscience at McGill University. You can reach me at emo4002@med.cornell.edu.
